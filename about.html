About Me

Let me tell you a little about myself...

My name is Michelle Ersan and I am from Munich, Germany.

After I completed my apprenticeship in Hotel Management, I traveled to Australia in October 2015. I fell in love with Melbourne and it's cultural diversity and decided to stay for good. I received my Permanent Residency in March 2020 and live since in the western suburbs with my partner and our Mini Lop rabbit, Harry Hopper.

I worked in hospitality for seven years when I came across UX design and coding and have been very passionate about it ever since. I started working towards a career as a Web Developer by taking online classes and teaching myself the fundamentals. After a few months, I successfully got a job with Parable Productions.

Not a day has passed I haven't been reading articles, looking for new projects to build and research how to improve myself as a person and developer.

"Let go of yesterday. Let today be a new beginning and be the best that you can, and you'll get to where you want to be." - Joel Osteen
